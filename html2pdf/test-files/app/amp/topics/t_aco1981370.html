Getting a Personal Auto Quote

About this task

The Personal auto quote submission process is managed by the Personal auto Quote Request wizard.

This topic goes through each step of the wizard, explaining what is required to get a quote and buy a new policy.

The Quote Request wizard starts with the Your Info step where you provide your personal details, such as name, address, email address, and the date the coverage is due to begin. When you get a quote from CustomerEngage Account Management, most of the Your Information fields are pre-filled with information taken from your account information in CustomerEngage Account Management.



You can accept the information provided or edit it, if required. You can also change the date when the coverage of the policy would begin.

Procedure

  1. Go the information fields you want to modify and update them as required.
  2. Modify the date in the I'd like my coverage to start on field if you want to change the effective date for when the policy is to begin.

    You can use the date/time picker or enter the date directly in the field. At any stage of the Quote Request wizard, you can go back to change the date when the policy comes into effect.

  3. Click Next to continue.
    At the top of the Quote Request wizard page, a new info banner appears that shows information that you provide while completing the steps of the quote request. The information in the Quote Request info banner includes the following:

    Info Field

    Description

    Auto Insurance Quote

    The quote number assigned to you after you enter your personal information. Keep note of this number because you can use it to return to the submission at any time by using Retrieve a Quote on the CustomerEngage Quote and Buy home page.

    Location

    The address you provided in the Your Info step.

    Vehicles

    The names of the vehicles you add in the Vehicle step. A number icon appears above the vehicles list indicating how many are added to the quote request.

    Drivers

    The names of the drivers added in Drivers step. A number icon appears above the drivers list indicating how many are added to the quote request.

    After you have completed the Your Information and For Your Quote sections, you are ready to answer the qualification questions in the Qualification step.

  4. Answer Qualification Questions.

    In the Qualification step, you answer questions about possible violations or declined insurance coverage that help the insurance company determine the risk factors for insuring you. An unsuitable answer can raise an underwriting issue that can potentially increase the risk for the insurer and prevent the customer from getting a quote online. The qualification questions are configurable.

    1. Select either Yes or No to answer each question on the page, or choose options from the drop-down list.

      In the case of a question like the following, when you click Yes a text field displays:

      Have you or any other driver been convicted for moving traffic violations within the past 3 years? If 'Yes' please explain.

    2. Click Next to continue.
      After you have completed the Qualification step, you are ready to add drivers’ details to the quote in the Drivers step.
  5. Add drivers to the quote.
    1. Enter details for the primary driver, such as gender, license number, year first licensed, license state, number of accidents, and violations.
    2. Verify the information in the First Name, Last Name, and Date of Birth fields. These fields are automatically populated from the Your Info step completed earlier.
    3. If required, you can add other drivers to the quote by clicking Add Driver in the Add Another Driver? section and adding their details:

      If you are using the mobile version of the CustomerEngage Account Management application, you can press Scan driver license barcode to get the information from it filled in automatically.

    4. Click the trash can icon to delete the driver’s details from the quote request.

      A dialog appears asking you to confirm that you want to remove the driver’s details from the quote request.

      After you have completed the Drivers step, you are ready to add vehicles to the quote in the Vehicles step.
  6. Add vehicles to the quote.

    In the Vehicles step, enter the vehicle information for each covered vehicle. In the reference implementation the fields provided are for VIN, Make, Model, Year, License Plate, State, and Cost New. After you have entered the vehicle’s make, model and year, a representative image of a vehicle matching these details is presented, if available, to confirm that you are getting a quote for the right vehicle:



    For more information about the technical details of the vehicle image display, see “Replacing Sample Lookup Plugins” in the Developer's Guide.

    You can also add additional interests to a vehicle on this step. This is typically a third party that has an interest in seeing that the coverage is valid and adequate, for example a bank who lent money for a car.

    1. Click Add Additional Interests and select a type - either Person or Company.
    2. Fill in the required details.

    Once you have added the vehicle or vehicles, click Next to continue to the next page and view the quote.

  7. Select quote offerings and coverages.

    The Quote step provides a quote for the policy based on the information you entered in the previous steps.

    The quote is presented as three offerings with the price of each based on pre-selected coverages. Each offering has a Buy Now button that enables you to purchase the policy at the price presented. You can see the price of the policy both in monthly installments and if paid in full. You can modify each of the offerings in the Quote step.



    The Personal auto offerings available out of the box are the following:
    • Basic Program
    • Premium Program
    • Standard Program

    Each of these offerings provides different amount of protection based on pre-selected coverages. The Standard option provides more coverages than Basic, while Premium provides more coverages than Standard.

    Two categories of coverages are presented - General and Vehicle-specific. More information about each coverage is available when you click the info icon on the coverage.

    The General coverages provide policy-level coverages as follows:

    General Coverages

    Description

    Liability - Bodily Injury and Property Damage

    Provides coverage for some of the cost for physical injury or property damage you have caused. In the Auto Liability Package, select the value that you want to remain liable for.

    Mexico Coverage - Limited

    Provides coverage for an incident that happens while travelling in Mexico. The claimant must be within a certain distance of the US border.

    Medical Payments

    Provides coverage for medical expenses for you and your passengers if you get involved in an accident.

    Underinsured Motorist - Property Damage

    Provides coverage against financial loss due to damage to your property by a motorist who is not adequately insured.

    Uninsured Motorist - Bodily Injury

    Provides coverage in the event that you are physically injured by a motorist who is not insured.

    Uninsured Motorist - Property Damage

    Provides coverage if you get hit by a motorist who does not have insurance, up to a certain limit.

    The Vehicle-specific coverages provide vehicle-level coverages as follows:

    Vehicle Coverages

    Description

    Collision

    Covers the damage to your car after a collision with another vehicle or object. The Collision deductible is the amount that you agree to pay out of pocket before the protection covers the rest.

    Comprehensive

    Coverage that pays for damages to your vehicle resulting from a covered loss other than a collision, such as fire, theft, or vandalism. You must pick a deductible, which is the amount that you agree to pay out of pocket when you have a claim.

    Rental Reimbursement

    Reimbursement for the cost of renting a vehicle to temporarily replace a vehicle that requires repairs following an incident.

    Towing and Labor

    Coverage for towing your vehicle and labor to repair it, limited by the Towing and Labor Limit. Select from the options available in a drop-down list.

  8. View and edit a quote.

    When a quote exists, you can edit the coverages included and recalculate the quote, request a quote to be sent by email, and purchase a policy for the quote.

    1. Select coverages to add or remove from the quote.

      The Buy Now button is replaced by a Recalculate button.

    2. Click Recalculate.

      The price of the quote is recalculated and the new price is shown.

      You can click Reset Coverages if you want to go back to the initial state of the quote.

  9. Request an email quote.
    1. View the reminder with the quote number. This indicates the quote information has been saved.


    2. You can have the quote sent to you by clicking Email Quote.
  10. (Optional) Print a quote.

    You can print the quote by clicking Print Quote. Note that you must enable the Background graphics option in your printer settings to ensure that the checkboxes of selected coverages are printed in the PDF.

  11. Buy the policy by clicking the Buy Now button for the offering you want to buy.
The next Additional Information step is available only if you have enabled the two-phase quoting feature. The feature is not enabled in the default configuration. For more information, see the Developer's guide.
  1. Provide additional information required for binding the quote and click Next.
    The information required for binding might be different for the different insurers. The driver and vehicle details that appear on the Additional Information page are configurable. For more information, see the Developer's Guide.
  2. View and edit policy information.

    The Policy Information step displays the details on the policy for review, including the vehicles and drivers that are covered. This step also gives you the option to update your contact details.

    When you choose to buy a specific quote, the Quote Request wizard displays the associated policy details in this step. For Personal auto, the information includes sections detailing the Vehicles, Drivers, and Primary Driver Contact Details.

    1. Review the policy details.
    2. Edit the primary driver contact details as needed.
    3. Click Next to continue.
      After you have completed the Policy Information step, you are ready to enter the payment information in the Payment Details step.
  3. Enter payment details.

    The Payment Details step enables you to choose a payment plan, a payment method, and to enter payment details. The average monthly premium and the total cost of the policy are presented.

    1. Review the list of payment plans that the billing system provides.

      These plan options are presented in rows under the headings, Name, Down Payment, Installment, and Total.

    2. Click the radio button beside the option to select the payment schedule you want to follow.
    3. Under Payment Options choose either Credit Card or Bank Account to make a payment.
  4. Select Bank Account or Credit Card from the drop-down menu and fill in the required fields:
    • Bank Account
      1. Click Checking or Savings in the Account Type field.
      2. Enter the Account Number.
      3. Enter the Routing (ABA) Number.
      4. Enter the name of the bank in the Bank Name field.
    • Credit Card
      1. Select the name of the card issuer for the credit card.
      2. Enter the credit card number in the Credit card number field.
      3. Enter the Expiration Date by selecting the month and the year from the drop-down menus.
  5. Click Buy Now.
    After you have completed the Payment Details step, you are ready to view the purchased policy information in the Purchase Confirmation step.
  6. View purchase confirmation.

    The confirmation screen provides you with the details of your new policy. It also displays the driver, vehicle, coverage, premium, and contact details for the policy.

Results

After you have completed the Purchase Confirmation step, you have finished the steps used to obtain a personal auto quote.

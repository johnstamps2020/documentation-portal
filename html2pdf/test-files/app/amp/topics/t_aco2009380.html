Provide Personal Information

About this task

The Your Info step contains two sections, Your Information and For Your Quote.

In the Your Info step you provide your email address, the address of the home that you want to insure and the date the coverage is due to begin. However, instead of you manually entering this information, your email and address details are already added to the Your Information fields for you. This information is taken from your account information in PolicyCenter. As you are already an authenticated user in the CustomerEngage Account Management application and PolicyCenter, the CustomerEngage Quote and Buy application is able to access the information in PolicyCenter on your behalf.



You can accept the information provided or edit it, if required. You can also change the date when the coverage of the policy would begin.

Procedure

  1. Update your email, if needed.
  2. Update the address fields in the Your Information section as needed or click Back to Address Search to search for another address.
    1. If you want to search for another address, begin entering the address in the Home Address field and click Search.
      If the address is found, you can select it from the list. If it is not found, click Enter your address manually to fill in the address in the form.
  3. Click the calendar icon for I'd like my coverage to start on to open the date/time picker and select the date when you want the coverage to start on.

    Alternatively, click the field to make it editable and enter the date and time.

  4. Click Next to continue.
    At the top of the Quote Request wizard page, a new info banner appears that shows information that you provide while completing the steps of the quote request. The information in the Quote Request info banner includes the following.

    Info Field

    Description

    Homeowners Insurance Quote

    The quote number assigned to you after you enter your personal information. Keep note of this number because you can use it to return to the submission at any time by using Retrieve a Quote on the CustomerEngage Quote and Buy home page.

    Location

    The address you provided in the Your Info step.

What to do next

After you have completed the Your Information and For Your Quote sections, you are ready to answer the qualification questions in the Qualification step.

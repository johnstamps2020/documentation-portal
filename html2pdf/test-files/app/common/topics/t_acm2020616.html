Filing a Broken Glass Claim

Before you begin

You have selected Personal auto as a policy type at the Select Policy step in the New Claim wizard.

Procedure

  1. At the Type of Incident step, click the Broken Glass tile and click Next.
  2. At the Damage step, specify where the accident happened using one of the following options:
    • If you know the exact address, enter the information in the respective fields.
    • If you just know the city, enter information for the City and State.
    • Choose the location on the map.

      If you choose this option, the map shows your current location by default. You can move the pin on the map to adjust the location.

    Important:

    The Google Maps-powered "Show (and Choose) a location on a map" is an optional feature and is considered a reference implementation only. The primary method to provide loss location information is by manually entering the address.

    If this optional "Show and Choose" feature is valuable to an insurer, then the insurer needs to license the Google Map API as appropriate. For this particular reference implementation, Guidewire is not in a position to recommend the appropriate license. However, it is our understanding that the usage in the CustomerEngage Account Management application does not fit the profile of free access as described in Google's license terms (Feb 16, 2016). Please contact Google to determine the appropriate license. For more information see: https://developers.google.com/maps/terms.

  3. Select the vehicle involved from the drop-down list.
  4. Describe what happened and click Next.
The next step assumes that you have enabled the Repair Facilities Selection capability in the ../applications/<application-name>/src/config/config.json5 file.
  1. At the Repair step, specify whether or not your car needs a repair using one of the following options:
    • Select I want to use a recommended repair facility and click Next.

      At the Repair Facility step, a map is presented giving you the option of looking for recommended repair facilities either near the policy address, near the location of the accident, or near your current location, if you have allowed the sharing of your location. You can select one either from the map or from the list besides. The repair facilities rating is also shown.

    • Select Iâ€™ll provide repair facility details myself and click Next.

      At the Repair Facility step you need to specify the details about your preferred repair facility, such as its name, address and contact information.

    • Select I donâ€™t need a repair facility if this is your case and click Next.
  2. Optional: At the Additional Information step, provide additional information by uploading supporting documents as part of the claim, such as images, police reports, or details of the original condition of the vehicle.

    The files you upload appear under the heading Claim Documents.

  3. Optional: At the Additional Information step, add information about witnesses or other third parties that may be involved and click Next.
  4. At the Contact Details step, provide information about the main contact for the claim.

    You can add either a person who is on the policy or another person. If you select a person on the policy, then the fields are pre-populated with the data from the policy, and the contact personâ€™s address cannot be modified. However, you can change the Home Phone number, Work Phone number, Mobile Phone number, and Email address. You can also choose which of the phone numbers is the Primary Phone to contact the claimant.

  5. At the Summary step, review the claim information you have provided and click Submit Claim unless you want to make any changes.

    You can return to any of the steps in the wizard to modify the information in the claim by clicking the title of the step in the wizard step list.

    Apart from the details about the claim and any repair facilities selected, the Summary step might display a fraud statement informing the user of the legal consequences of providing untrue information in a claim.

    The content of this statement is configurable according to the insurersâ€™ requirements.

Results

When the submission is successful, the Confirmation page is presented with the claim reference number.

An insurer can choose to provide other information, such as contact information for the adjuster or next steps. The additional information provided in the Confirmation page is dependent on the insurerâ€™s business requirements.

The screen also displays a service level agreement, which can be modified based on the insurerâ€™s requirements.

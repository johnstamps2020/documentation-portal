BillingAPI Invoice preview web services

In 9.0, the BillingAPI web service has renamed the two similarly named methods used to preview the effects of a payment plan on a policy.

Renamed method previewInstallmentsPlanInvoices

The previewInstallmentsPlanInvoices method has been renamed to previewIssuanceInvoices. The renamed method’s argument and return value remain unchanged. The method determines the effects of a payment plan on a new policy.

Renamed method previewInstallmentPlanInvoices

The previewInstallmentPlanInvoices method has been renamed to previewPolicyChangeInvoices. The renamed method’s argument and return value remain unchanged. The method determines the effects of a payment plan on an existing policy.

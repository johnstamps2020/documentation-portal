The Account Details screen

Name

Description
Account Details toolbar
Close Account button Close the account. For more information, see Closing accounts.
Start Delinquency button Start a delinquency for the account. For more information, see Starting delinquencies manually.
Edit button Edit the account.
Account Basics section
Account # The account's account number.
Account Name The account name.
Type

The account's type. In the base configuration, the possible values are:

  • Insured
  • List Bill
  • Payer
  • Collection Agency
Parent Account The account's parent account, if any.
Currency The account's currency.
Doing Business As For commercial accounts, the name of the business listed on the account's policies. This is typically specified only if the business uses a name different from the name of the account. This is typically not specified for personal accounts.
Segment

The account's business segment. In the base configuration, the possible values are:

  • Large Business
  • Medium Business
  • Small Business
  • Personal
  • Subprime
Creation Date The date the account was created.
Close Date The date the account was closed. This is visible only if the account has been closed.
Service Tier

The account's service tier. In the base configuration, the possible values are:

  • Platinum
  • Gold
  • Silver
Security Zone The account's security zone.
Contact Info section
Primary Contact The account's primary contact.
Address The postal address of the account's primary contact.
Phone The phone number of the account's primary contact.
Email The email of the account's primary contact.
Company Type section
Organization Type If the account is a company, the account's organization type, such as corporation, partnership, or government entity. Note that this is a text field into which you type a value and not a drop-down list.
FEIN If the account is a company, the account's Federal Employee Identification Number.
Account Totals section
Total Unbilled

The amount on all charges owned by the account that is neither billed nor paid.

(This total does not include amounts that have been billed or amounts that have not been billed but have been paid in advance. It includes only amount that have not been billed and not been paid.)
Total Currently Billed The amount billed on all charges owned by the account that is neither paid nor past due.
Total Past Due The amount past due on all charges owned by the account.
Total Outstanding The amount billed or past due on all charges owned by the account that is not paid.
Policy Totals section The values in this section exclude account-level charges, such as payment reversal fees.
Total Unbilled

The amount on all policy-level charges owned by the account that is neither billed nor paid. This excludes account-level charges, such as payment reversal fees.

(This total does not include amounts that have been billed or amounts that have not been billed but have been paid in advance. It includes only amount that have not been billed and not been paid.)
Total Currently Billed The amount billed on all policy-level charges owned by the account that is neither paid nor past due.
Total Past Due The amount past due on all policy-level charges owned by the account.
Total Outstanding The amount billed or past due on all policy-level charges owned by the account that is not paid.
Total Paid The amount paid on all policy-level charges owned by the account.
Total Value The total value of all policies.
Unapplied Funds section
Unapplied Amount The total amount of money in all unapplied funds owned by the account.
Suspense Item Amount The total amount of money in all suspense items owned by the account.
Collateral section
Collateral Status The account's collateral status. The text is a link you can click to view the Collateral screen.
Payment Allocation section
Payment Allocation Plan The account's payment allocation plan. The text is a link you can click to view details about the payment allocation plan.
Billing Info section
Billing Plan The account's billing plan. The text is a link you can click to view details about the billing plan.
Fix Bill Date/Due Date

The field is visible for account-level billing accounts only.

Whether the account uses bill date invoicing or due date invoicing.
Account-Level Charge Date sections This label is visible for policy-level billing accounts only.
Fix Bill Date/Due Date

The field is visible for policy-level billing accounts only.

Whether the invoices for account-level charges use bill date invoicing or due date invoicing.
Day of Month

The field is visible for policy-level billing accounts only.

The anchor date for the invoice stream that contains invoices used to bill account-level charges.
Invoicing Dates section This label is visible for account-level billing accounts only.
Monthly Policies subsection This label is visible for account-level billing accounts only.
Invoice Day of Month

The field is visible for account-level billing accounts only.

The anchor date for the invoice stream that contains invoices for policies billed monthly.
Weekly Policies subsection This label is visible for account-level billing accounts only.
Day of Week

The field is visible for account-level billing accounts only.

The anchor day of week for the invoice stream that contains invoices for policies billed weekly.
Every Other Week Policies subsection This label is visible for account-level billing accounts only.
Anchor Date

The field is visible for account-level billing accounts only.

The anchor date for the invoice stream that contains invoices for policies billed every other week.
Twice Per Month Policies subsection This label is visible for account-level billing accounts only.
First Invoice Day of Month

The field is visible for account-level billing accounts only.

The first anchor date for the invoice stream that contains invoices for policies billed twice per month.
Second Invoice Day of Month

The field is visible for account-level billing accounts only.

The second anchor date for the invoice stream that contains invoices for policies billed twice per month.
Send Invoices By The channel used to send invoices to the account (such as Email or Mail).
Payment Instrument The account's payment instrument.
Supported Payment Plans

This field is visible for list bill accounts only.

A link to the List Bill Payment Plans screen, which lists the payment plans the account supports.
Billing Level section
Billing Level

The account's billing level.
Cash Separation

Whether the account uses cash separation or not. Note that cash separation is available for policy-level billing accounts only.
Write-Offs section
Write-Off Amount The amount that has been written off on all charges owned by the account.
Delinquency section
Delinquent Amount The total amount of all active delinquencies for the account.
Delinquency Plan The account's delinquency plan.
Collection Agency

The field is visible for accounts with active delinquencies only.

The collection agency associated with the account, if any.
Open Policy Status list
Policy Number The policy's policy number.
Product The policy's product (such as personal auto or homeowner's).
Payment Status This is either the value Good Standing (if there is no money past due) or the value Past Due (if there is money past due).
Effective Date The policy period's effective date.
Expiration Date The policy period's expiration date.
Adj. Unbilled The adjusted unbilled amount. This is the total unpaid amount for invoice items owned by this policy period and placed on planned invoices.
Adj. Current The adjusted current amount. This is the total unpaid amount for invoice items owned by this policy period and placed on billed invoices. (This does not include invoice items on due invoices.)
Past Due The amount past due.
Outstanding The total adjusted outstanding amount. This is the total unpaid amount for invoice items owned by this policy period and placed on billed and due invoices.
Paid The amount paid.
Total Value The total value of the policy.
Recent Payments Received list
Actions menu

Modify the payment. The options include changing the distribution, moving the payment to another account or suspense, or reversing the payment.

If the payment has been reversed, the menu is replaced with a Reversed label.
Payment Date The payment date.
Reversed If the payment was reversed, the payment reversal date.
Payment Instrument The payment's payment instrument.
Check/Ref # The payment's check number or reference number.
Unapplied Fund The unapplied fund into which the payment was initially deposited.
Amount The payment amount.
Amount Distributed The amount of the payment that has been distributed.
Policy The target policy for the payment. There is a value only if the payment was targeted for a specific policy, such as when entered through the Multiple Payment Entry Wizard.
Invoice The target invoice for the payment. There is a value only if the payment was targeted for a specific invoice, such as when entered through the Multiple Payment Entry Wizard.
Suspense The amount of the payment that has been put into suspense items.
Collateral The amount of the payment that has been put into collateral.
Frozen

This field is visible only when archiving has been enabled.

Whether the payment is frozen.

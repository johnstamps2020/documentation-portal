Billing Level Settings section

Policy Level Billing: Fix Bill Date/Due Date & Item Placement Cutoff Date

For policy-level billing accounts, this field controls two behaviors: whether invoices are modeled around bill date or due date, and the item placement cutoff date.

  • When set to Fix Bill Date, for each invoice:
    • The invoice bill date is set to the invoice item placement date.
    • The invoice due date is determined by adding the lead time to the bill date.
    • The cutoff date for the invoice is its bill date.
  • When set to Fix Due Date:
    • The invoice due date is set to the invoice item placement date.
    • The invoice bill date is determined by subtracting the lead time from the due date.
    • The cutoff date for each invoice item is its due date.

Account-Level Billing/Agency Billing: Fix Bill Date/Due Date

The invoice cutoff date for accounts with account-level billing or agency billing.

The amount of the installment fee to add to an invoice that has a down payment or installment invoice item.

Min # Equity Days

The default value for the preferred equity threshold for policy periods created from this payment plan. Note that after a given policy period has been created, the threshold for that policy period can be modified regardless of the payment plan setting.

For more information, see Maintaining positive equity.

Changes to BillingCenter batch processes

Guidewire has made the following changes to batch processing in BillingCenter 9.0.0.

New archive-related batch processes

As of BillingCenter 9.0.0, Guidewire supports the archiving of PolicyPeriod objects. To facilitate archiving, Guidewire has added the following archive-related batch processes to Guidewire BillingCenter:

  • Archive – Archives policy period objects
  • Retrieve Policy Period – Retrieves archived policy period objects

Legacy letter of credit batch process removed

Guidewire has removed the Legacy Letter of Credit batch process. Use Legacy Collateral batch process instead.

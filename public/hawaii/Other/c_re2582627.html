Handling visibility of coverages

As a result of modularizing lines of business, the way PolicyCenter handles visibility of coverages has changed. Previously, all coverages were discovered in an iterator and the visible property of PCF pages was used to render available coverages. Beginning with this release, the needed coverages are selected within the iterator and then all available coverages are rendered without using the visible property.

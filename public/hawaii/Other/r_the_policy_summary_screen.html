The Policy Summary screen

Name

Description
Request Retrieve button

This button is visible only if the policy period has been archived and the user has permission to retrieve policy periods. For more information, see Archiving policies.

Request retrieval of a policy from the archive.
Start Delinquency button Start a delinquency for the policy period. For more information, see Starting delinquencies manually.
Details button View the Policy Details screen.
Overview section
Insured

The policy period's named insured. This is the name of the policy period contact with the role of Primary Insured, if any.

The value is a link you can click to view the Contacts screen.
Policy Lifetime The length of time the policy has been in force, including all previous policy periods.

When a policy is older than one year, an icon is shown before the lifetime description.

Effective Dates The effective and expiration dates of the policy period.
Cancellation Status

The cancellation status of the policy. The possible values are:

  • Open - This means the policy period is in good standing.
  • Pending Cancellation - A request to cancel the policy has been sent to the policy administration system.
  • Pending Rescind Cancellation - A request to cancel the policy has been sent to the policy administration system. A second request has been sent to rescind the cancellation request.
  • Canceled - The policy has been canceled by the policy administration system.
  • Pending Reinstatement - A request to reinstate the policy has been sent to the policy administration system.
Delinquencies

The number of delinquencies the policy period has had in the past 12 months.

If the policy period is currently delinquency, a delinquency icon (a calendar with an !) appears before the number of delinquencies.
UW Company The policy's underwriting company.
Currency The policy's currency
Billing Method The policy's billing method (direct bill, list bill, or agency bill).
Send Invoices By The channel used to send invoices to the account (such as Email or Mail).
Default Payment Method The account's payment instrument, which is used by default to pay for the policy period.
Payment Plan The policy period's payment plan.
Notes section This section displays the most recently created policy note. If the policy has no notes, BillingCenter shows text stating "There are no notes on this policy.".
+ button Add a new note to the account.
Pencil icon Edit the most recent note
Most Recent Note Click the label to view the Policy Notes screen, which lets a user search for and view all of the policy's notes.
Date The date of the most recent note. If the policy has no notes, this field is not visible.
Topic The topic of the most recent note. If the policy has no notes, this field is not visible.
Subject The subject of the most recent note. If the policy has no notes, this field is not visible.
Text The text of the most recent note. If the policy has no notes, this field is not visible.

Financials section

This section is visible when the account uses policy-level billing and the policy period is not archived.
Current Payment Expected

The policy's current outstanding amount. This is the amount that has been billed and not yet paid.

The value is a link you can click to view the Payment Schedule screen.
Past Due

The amount on the policy that is past due.

If the amount past due is greater than zero, a past due icon (a red clock) appears before the past due amount.
Late Fees The total of all late fees that are unpaid. If there are late fees, there is an additional control that links to the Policy Late Fee Reversal wizard.
Next Invoice Due

The amount due on the next invoice to become due.

The value is a link you can click to view the Payment Schedule screen with the Payment Schedule tab active.
Last Payment Received

The amount of the last payment received. The date the payment was received is included in the field label.

The value is a link you can click to view the Payment Schedule screen with the Payment History tab active.

If no payments have been received, then the label reads No Payment Received.
"The policy is paid at the account level." This label is visible if the policy's billing method is direct bill and the policy's account uses account-level billing.
"The policy is paid at the producer level." This label is visible if the policy's billing method is agency bill.

Pie chart section
Pie chart

The pie chart displays the money for the policy period divided into the following categories:

  • Unbilled
  • Paid
  • Written Off
  • Billed
  • Past Due
Policy Value The total value of the policy period.
Equity section
Policy Equity The financial amount of service that has been paid for and not yet provided.
Equity Percent The policy equity from the previous field divided by the total policy premium, expressed as a percent. Non-premium charges (such as taxes and fees) are not included when determining the equity percent.
Paid Through The date on which the policy equity drops to or below zero if no further payments are made.
Unapplied Fund section
Designated Unapplied

The total amount of money in the policy period's designated unapplied fund.

This field appears only if the account that owns the policy uses policy-level billing with cash separation.
Total Unapplied

For the account that owns the policy, the total amount of money in its default unapplied fund and all of its designated unapplied funds. The value is a link you can click to view the Unapplied Funds Details screen.

This field appears only if the account that owns the policy uses policy-level billing with cash separation.
Default Unapplied

For the account that owns the policy, the amount of money in the account's default unapplied fund. The value is a link you can click to view the Unapplied Funds Details screen.

This field appears only if the account:

  • Uses account-level billing.
  • Uses policy-level billing without cash separation.

"The policy is paid at the producer level." This label is visible if the policy's billing method is agency bill.
Premium Charges The total of the policy period's premium charges.
Other charges The total of the policy period's non-premium charges.
Total The total of all of the policy period's charges.
Paid The amount of the policy that was paid.
Written Off The amount of the policy that was written off.

This field appears only if the account that owns the policy uses policy-level billing with cash separation.
Default Unapplied

Create visualized product directly in APD

If you do not have access to XMind, you can create a visualized product directly in APD, without starting from a mind map.

Before you begin

  • Set Product Design Mode to Design or above. See Set Product Design Mode
  • Navigate to the Visualized Products tab

About this task

These steps guide you through the process of creating a visualized product.

Procedure

  1. On the Visualized Products tab of the Product Management screen, select New Product.
    APD displays the Load Product Template screen.
  2. Enter values for Product fields as described in Product fields on Product Definition screen.
  3. Enter values for Product Line fields as described in Product line Details tab fields.
  4. When you have complete filling out product and line details, click Update.
    APD creates the visualized product and adds it to the table of visualized products.

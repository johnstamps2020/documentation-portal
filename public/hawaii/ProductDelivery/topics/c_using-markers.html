Markers identify product model elements

XMind provides Markers to prioritize, classify, and illustrate your mind map. Guidewire provides XMind markers to tag the elements of your map that are part of the product model and visualization in APD.

The following screen shot from XMind shows a product line with Guidewire markers attached to the line, risk object, coverages, and other parts of the product model. These markers provide information that APD uses when importing the map.

Other XMind markers

Besides the Guidewire markers and legacy markers, you can use other XMind markers, for any purpose, such as flagging items that need further review. However, these are ignored when importing into PolicyCenter.

Avoid using the legacy markers because they will be interpreted into product model elements by APD.
